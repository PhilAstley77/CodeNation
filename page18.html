1.5 Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

The main takeaway is that if you did not yourself write something, make a graphic, video or song or are using an excerpt from a book then you have to give credit where it is due, some sites are around to allow people to gain copyrighted photos such as Shutterstock where the original creator is paid. Copyright theft is still theft! To that end, the Matrix design is the copyright of Warner Bros.
